Food Recipe App

a react.js app

Introducing my latest project - a delicious food recipe app, built using React.js and an open API. This app features a convenient search bar, allowing users to easily find a variety of recipes. It also includes navigational links to different cuisines, such as American and Italian. Each recipe listed includes comprehensive information, including the steps to make the dish and the necessary ingredients. Whether you're a seasoned cook or just starting out, this app has everything you need to bring your culinary creations to life.

The technology stack for this application includes React.js on the client-side, and Open API. These technologies were carefully chosen to provide a seamless user experience, efficient data management, and real-time communication capabilities. Click below to see the code on github.
